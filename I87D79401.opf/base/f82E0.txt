§74 sarvajñajñānānubodhanasarvakuśaladharmābhinirhṛtaḥ sarvabodhipakṣyadharmasamudgamaḥ

ayaṃ sa kulaputra dharmayajñaḥ,yatra dharmayajñe pratiṣṭhitā bodhisatvā iṣṭayajñayājūkā dakṣiṇīyā bhavanti sadevakasya lo
kasya |evaṃ hi bhagavan tasya gṛhapater nirdiśatas tasyā brāhmaṇaparṣado dvayor brāhmaṇaśatayor anuttarāyāṃ samyakṣaṃbod
hau cittāny utpannāni |
