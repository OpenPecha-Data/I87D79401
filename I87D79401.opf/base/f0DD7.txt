§14 atha vimalakīrtir licchavir jānann eva tān bhagavato gandhottamakūṭasya tathāgatasya buddhakṣetrād āgatān bodhisatvān
etad avocat:kidṛśī kulaputrāḥ gandhottamakūṭasya tathāgatasya dharmadeśanā |

ta evam āhuḥ:na sa tathāgato ’kṣaraniruktyā dharmaṃ deśayati |tenaiva gandhena te bodhisatvā vinayaṃ gacchanti |yatra yat
raiva gandhavṛkṣe te bodhisatvā niṣīdanti,tatas tatas tādṛśo gandhaḥ pravātiyat samanantarāghrāte gandhe sarvabodhisatva
guṇākaraṃ nāma samādhiṃ pratilabhanteyasya samādheḥ samanantarapratilambhāt sarveṣāṃ bodhisatvaguṇā jāyante |
