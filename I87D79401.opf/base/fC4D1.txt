§9 tataś caiko rājakumāraḥ somacchatro nāma |tasyaikākino rahogatasyaitad abhūt:asti tv asyāḥ pujāyā anyodāratā viśiṣṭata
rā pūjeti |

tasyāntarīkṣād devatā buddhādhiṣṭhānenaivam āha:dharmapūjā satpuruṣa sarvapūjābhyo viśiṣyate |

sa evam āha: katamā punaḥ sā dharmapūjeti |

devatāha: etam eva tvaṃ satpuruṣa tathāgataṃ bhaiṣajyarājam upasaṃkramya paripṛccha katamā sā dharmapūjeti |sa te bhagavā
n vyākariṣyati |

atha devānām indra sa somacchatro rājakumāro yena bhagavān bhaiṣajyarājas tathāgato ’rhan samyakṣaṃbuddhas tenopasaṃkrāma
t |upasaṃkramya tasya bhagavataḥ pādau śirasā vanditvaikānte ’sthāt |ekāntasthitaś ca somacchatro rājakumāras taṃ bhagav
antaṃ bhaiṣajyarājaṃ tathāgatam etad avocat:dharmapūjā dharmapūjeti bhagavan ucyate | katamā sā dharmapūjeti |
