§10 api tu khalu punar mañjuśrīḥ kathaṃ bodhisatvena glānako bodhisatvaḥ pratisaṃmoditavyaḥ |

mañjuśrīr āha: kāyānityatayā na ca nirvidvirāgatayā,kāyaduḥkhatayā na ca nirvāṇābhinandanatayā,kāyānātmatayā satvaparipāc
anatayā ca,kāyaśāntatayā na cātyantaśamatayā,sarvaduścaritadeśanatayā na ca saṃkrāntitaḥ,svaglānyena parasatvaglānakaruṇ
atayā,pūrvāntakoṭīduḥkhānusmaraṇatayā,satvārthakriyānusmaraṇatayā,kuśalamūlābhimukhīkaraṇatayā,ādiviśuddhatayā,aparitarṣ
aṇatayā,sadāvīryāraṃbhāc ca,vaidyarājo bhaviṣyasi sarvavyādhīnāṃ śamayiteti |evaṃ bodhisatvena bodhisatvo glānaḥ pratisa
ṃmoditavyaḥ |
