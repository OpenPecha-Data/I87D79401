§38 tatra bhagavān āyuṣmantaṃ rāhulam āmantrayate sma:gaccha tvaṃ rāhula vimalakīrter licchaver glānaparipṛcchakaḥ |

rāhulo ’py āha: nāhaṃ bhagavan utsahe tasya satpuruṣasya glānaparipṛcchako gantum |tat kasmād dhetoḥ |abhijānāmy ahaṃ bha
gavan: ekasmin samaye saṃbahulā licchavikumārakā yenāhaṃ tenopasaṃkramya mām etad avocan:tvaṃ rāhula tasya bhagavataḥ pu
traś cakravartirājyam utsṛjya pravrajitaḥtatra ke te pravrajyāyā guṇānuśaṃsāḥ |

teṣām ahaṃ yathārūpaṃ pravrajyāyā guṇānuśaṃsā nirdiśāmivimalakīrtir licchavir yenāhaṃ tenopasaṃkrāntaḥ | sa mām abhivandy
