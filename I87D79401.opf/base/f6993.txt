§13 atha tato bhojanāt sarvā sā parṣat tṛptā kṛtā |na ca tāvad bhojanaṃ kṣīyate |yaiś ca bodhisatvaiḥ śrāvakaiḥ śakrabrah
malokapālais tadanyaiś ca satvais tad bhojanaṃ bhuktam,teṣāṃ tādṛśaṃ sukhaṃ kāye ’vakrāntaṃ yādṛśaṃ sarvasukhapratimaṇḍi
te lokadhātau bodhisatvānāṃ sukham |sarvaromakūpebhyaś ca teṣāṃ tādṛśo gandhaḥ pravāti |tadyathāpi nāma tasminn eva sarv
agandhasugandhe lokadhātau vṛkṣāṇāṃ gandhaḥ |
