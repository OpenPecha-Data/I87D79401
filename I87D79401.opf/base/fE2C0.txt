§18 sarvadṛṣṭigateṣu cāryaman antargato na cāntamadhyaprāptaḥaṣṭākṣaṇasamavasaraṇaś cāsi na cāsi lakṣaṇam anuprāptaḥsaṃkl
eśena cāsi samo vyavadānam adhigataḥyā ca sarvasatvānām araṇā sā bhadantasyāpi araṇāna ca tvayā dakṣiṇā viśodhyateye ca 
bhadantāya piṇḍapātaṃ dadati tāṃś ca vinipātayasisarvamāraiś ca te sārdham ekahastaḥ kṛtaḥ sarvakleśāś ca te sahāyāḥyats
vabhāvāś ca kleśās tatsvabhāvo bhadantaḥsarvasatveṣu te vadhakacittaṃ pratyupasthitamsarvabuddhāś ca tebhyākhyātāḥ sarva
buddhadharmāṃś ca pratikrośasi na cāsi saṃghapratisaraṇaḥna ca jātu parinirvāsyasi |evaṃ tvam imaṃ piṇḍapātaṃ pratigṛhīṇ
